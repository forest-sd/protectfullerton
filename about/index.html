Who We Are

Protect Fullerton is a nonpartisan community organization founded by residents who care deeply about the future of our city. We believe that effective local governance requires active citizen participation, transparent decision-making, and elected officials who are accountable to the people they serve. We provide information, organize community forums, and advocate for policies that benefit all Fullerton residents.
